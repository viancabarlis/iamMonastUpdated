ABOUT US COMMISSIONS

COMMISSIONS


iamMonast offers two services:
  1. Pre-Built PCs and Mechanical Keyboards - clients will be able to choose from already built devices that iamMonast completed.
  2. Tailored Commission - iamMonast will construct a custom PC or mechanical keyboard based on your needs and budget.
  3. Build Service - assembly for clients who already picked PC or mechanical keyboard parts.


PROCESS

Tailored Commission
  1. iamMonast will work closely with the client to find the best parts for the price range while prioritizing their need.
  2. Cost of the parts must be paid upfront by the client.
  3. PC and/or keyboard are assembled live on Twitch and uploaded on YouTube.
  4. Invoice sent for assembly rate + return shipping (if necessary).
  5. Assembled PC and/or keyboard are shipped or picked up by the client.
Build Service
  1. Client gathers all the parts for a PC/keyboard build.
  2. Client sends all parts to iamMonast.
  3. The build process is streamed on Twitch and uploaded on YouTube.
  4. Invoice sent for assembly rate + return shipping (if necessary).
  5. Assembled PC and/or keyboard are shipped or picked up by the client.
